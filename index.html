About Us

How Our Journey Started

  • March 2009

    Started from the bottom

    When the paper giant, Dunder Mifflin, dominates over various paper companies in Scranton, EX-Regional Manager of Dunder Mifflin's Scranton Branch, Michael Scott, took it upon himself to start his own company that would rival that of Dunder Mifflin; Upon talks of how Dunder Mifflin mistreates their workers by not letting them have parties on their birthdays, Michael Scott decided that enough was enough and Dunder Mifflin has to be stopped.

  • March 2009

    "You miss 100% of the shots you don't take" - Michael Scott

    With almost no investment in his savings after leaving Dunder Mifflin and having decleared bankruptcy, literally, not so long ago, Michael was off to a rought start. But, it was his dream to work with paper and nothing was going to stop him from chasing his dream.

  • March 2009

    Dream Team

    After a "long and hard" journey of scounting for investors and potential employees, Michael was lucky enough to recruit artist Pam Beesly, a former receptionist that worked with Michael and Ryan Howard, an ambitious business student that recently got out of prison for unlawful business practices.

  • March 2009

    The Office

    Renting a spare room right beneath Dunder Mifflin, The Michael Scott Paper Company slowly takes off. Although they were barely getting any customers at first, Michael must get everyone to work together and be on the same page by coordinating throwing cheese puff balls into each other's mouths.

  • March 2004

    "You have no idea how high I can fly." - Michael Scott

    With Michael's decade of sales experience, he slowly steals clients right under Dunder Mifflin's nose and saved the company from going under completely... yet. Without a warehouse he would have to work a little harder to provide customers that same quality of service that he have done before. This is the start of something great.
